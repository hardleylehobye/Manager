If you discover a security vulnerability in this project, please report it responsibly by following these steps:

Report to:hardleylehobye@gmail.com

Response Time: We aim to respond within 48 hours of receiving a report.

Process:

We will acknowledge your report promptly.

Investigate the issue and, if valid, prepare a fix or mitigation.

Keep you updated regularly on the progress.

Once resolved, we will publicly disclose the issue in coordination with you.

If your report is declined: We will explain why and may offer suggestions for further steps.

Thank you for helping us keep the project secure!
